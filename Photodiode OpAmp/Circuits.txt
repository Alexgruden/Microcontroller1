Created 3 digrams in my notebook for this Lab:

1. A light activated circuit - Uses a transistor to activate a LED when there is light
2. A Darkness Detecting Circuit - detects when there is darkness and does the opposite of the light activated circuit.
3. Photodiote Amplifier Circuit - Photodiote module producing a small current connects to op amp. The tiny current is then
amplified to be able to be read. The bias section makes sure the whole thing works properly on a single supply.
