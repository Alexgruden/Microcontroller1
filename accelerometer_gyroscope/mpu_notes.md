## MPU-6050

On the chip:
- 3 axis gyroscope
- 3 axis accelerometer

- The gyroscope measures:
- rotational velocity or rate of change of the angular position over time
- along the X, Y and Z axis.

### It uses MEMS technology and the Coriolis Effect for measuring
