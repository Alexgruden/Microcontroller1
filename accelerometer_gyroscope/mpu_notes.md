## MPU-6050

On the chip:
- 3 axis gyroscope
- 3 axis accelerometer

- The gyroscope measures:
- rotational velocity or rate of change of the angular position over time
- along the X, Y and Z axis.

### It uses MEMS technology and the Coriolis Effect for measuring
Information About it is as follows:
- MEMS = Micro-Electrical-Systems: they integrate mechanical structures and electronics,
- Accelerometer = It uses a small mass on a spring between fixed capacitor plates; when the device accelerates, the mass shifts, changing capacitance, which is processed into an acceleration value along each axis.
howtomechatronics
- Gyroscope =  It relies on the Coriolis effect; an oscillating mass experiences a perpendicular displacement when the device rotates, changing capacitance that is converted to angular rate (degrees per second) on each axis
